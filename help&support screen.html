Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the "Account Settings" page and click on "Reset Password".

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, browse the course catalog, select a course, and click "Enroll".

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses. For more information, visit our refund policy page.
